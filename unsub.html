Manage email subscriptions

Enter the email shown below. You can unsubscribe/resubscribe the entire email channel or update specific groups.

The email is fetched from the unsubscribe link / CleverTap context.

Full-channel actions

Full-channel actions call $WZRK_WR.unSubEmail / $WZRK_WR.subEmail. If the SDK isn't present, a fallback to server endpoints is attempted.

Group-level preferences

Group updates call $WZRK_WR.changeSubscriptionGroups. The UI shows each group and whether it's currently unsubscribed.
